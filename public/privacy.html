Privacy Policy

Last updated: 04/07/2024

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Registration Data: When you register, we collect your Telegram username.
  • User Interaction Data: This includes your messages, meeting schedules, and feedback shared through KarmaBot.

Non-Personal Information:

  • Usage Data: We may collect data on how you use our bot, such as interaction timestamps, features accessed, and logs of your activities.
  • Technical Data: Information such as your device type, operating system, and other technical details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To Provide and Improve Services: Facilitate communication between job seekers and recruiters, track commitments and send automated reminders, and improve the functionality and performance of KarmaBot.
  • Communication: Send you updates, newsletters, and other information related to our services.
  • Compliance: Comply with legal obligations and resolve disputes.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

  • With Your Consent: We will share your information with third parties if you give us explicit permission.
  • Service Providers: We may share your information with third-party service providers who assist us in operating our services, provided they adhere to strict data protection and confidentiality agreements.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is completely secure.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Object to the processing of your data.
  • Withdraw your consent at any time, where applicable.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@karma-bot.com.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on our website and updating the "Last Updated" date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@karma-bot.com.
